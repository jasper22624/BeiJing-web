Great Wall

Tiananmen Square, located in the heart of Beijing, is one of the largest public squares in the world and a significant cultural and historical site in China. Covering about 44 hectares, it is surrounded by notable landmarks, including the Tiananmen Gate, the Monument to the People's Heroes, and the Mausoleum of Mao Zedong.

Tiananmen Square continues to be a place of national celebration, hosting events such as the annual National Day ceremonies.

Visitors can explore its vast space, reflecting on its rich history and the complex narratives associated with it.
